每日英語：Afive-weekoldfilly...

Afive-weekoldfilly（小雌馬）clonedfromafamouscuttinghorse（趕牛馬）,RoyalBlueBoon,raninOklahomaonThursday,withthemare（母馬）thatcarriedtheclonedembryo（複製胚胎）andgavebirth（分娩）inanormalway.TwoU.Sbiotechnologycompaniessuccessfullyclonedtwocuttinghorses,whichwereborninFebruaryandMarch.Fivemoreclonesareexpectedthisyear.　AP

複製自著名趕牛馬RoyalBlueBoon的五周大雌性小馬，周四跟母馬在俄克拉何馬州草地上奔馳。RoyalBlueBoon分娩前懷着的是複製胚胎，其後正常分娩。美國兩間生物技術公司分別在二月和三月，成功複製兩隻趕牛馬，預計今年還有五隻複製馬誕生。
